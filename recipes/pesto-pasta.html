Pesto Pasta

Enjoy this authentic pesto pasta!

Easy, fast and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Pasta, your favorite kind.
  • Onion and oil.
  • Pesto, store bought or homemade.
  • Salt and pepper.
  • Parmesam cheese, shredded.

Steps

  1. Boil your favorite pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the basil pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
  4. Add some extra cheese and basil as topping.
